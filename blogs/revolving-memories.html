I can't talk about it much due to wanting the Lore to be: Somewhat Community Driven / Revealed in the Game, (And in other places.)

Anyway in LOTSM - Revolving Memories: You play as Nicholas a Spirit Fox That lost His Memory, He only Remembers a few Names: Nicholas, Fia, Stella, And Luna (Equivalent to, in order: Spirit Fox, Snow Wolf, Stellar Fairy, Soul Mage).

You can swap between Ability sets, (Being Nicholas's Friends Abilitys) The first Friend you find is Fia who has the Abilities of: Double Jump And Dash.
